# Roman Shades

## Style Variants

- **Standard Roman Shades** — flat-fold construction; available in both drilled (bracket-mounted) and No-Drill installation.
- **Relaxed Roman Shades** — softer, draped bottom edge for a more casual look (e.g. Belgian linen styles).
- **Trimmed Roman Shades** — standard construction with a decorative border/trim along the edge.

## Customizable Range

Maximum width and length vary by specific design — narrower styles cap around 55" wide / 78" long, wider styles up to 106" wide / 86" long. Where a cordless lift is selected, width is capped lower than the motorized/corded maximum (commonly ~86" even on designs rated to 106").

## Mounting & Lift

| Attribute | Options |
|---|---|
| Mount | No-drill, or wall/ceiling bracket mount |
| Lift | Cordless or motorized (varies by design; some styles are cordless-only) |
| Control | Manual or motorized |

## Fabric Weight & Light Blocking

| Spec | Range |
|---|---|
| Fabric weight | 107–478 GSM |
| Light blocking | 15%–100% |

## Safety Certification

Curtarra states its shades are cordless and meet the ANSI/WCMA A100.1-2018 safety standard.

## Lining Options

White Lining (140 GSM, ~30% light-blocking), Cotton Lining (137 GSM, ~30%), White Blackout Lining (240 GSM, 100%), or unlined. Same lining products documented in [Lining Type](../guides/lining-type.md). Lining adds privacy, light control, insulation, and UV protection for the face fabric, at the cost of a bulkier stack back.
