# Custom Pillow Covers

Custom-made pillow/cushion covers offered as a companion product to Curtarra's curtain fabric lines (e.g. Abe, Auden, Bellina, Callie, Freya, Langley, Sabrina, Victoria, and others), letting customers match throw pillows to their curtain fabric.

## Formats

Sold as single pieces or pairs; some fixed-size options (e.g. 18"×18") in addition to made-to-measure.
