# Custom Curtains

## Customizable Range

| Dimension | Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Width | Solid fabric: no hard limit (splice seam added over 104"). Patterned fabric: same splicing rule applies. | Per-product pages often default to showing 360" as the practical max — that's a display convention, not a hard ceiling for solid fabric. |
| Length | Solid fabric: no hard limit (splice seam over 104"). Patterned fabric: capped at 104" unless the pattern is rotated 90°, which extends the usable length to 240". | Full mechanism: [Curtain Sizing & Customization FAQ](../faq/curtain-customization-faq.md) |
| Dimensional tolerance | ±1" | Handmade product |

Order quantity = number of single panels (quantity 1 gives one panel, not a pair).

Exact per-fabric width/length limits are set at the SKU level — always confirm on the specific product page before quoting a customer an exact number.

## Fabric Weight & Light Blocking (across the curtain line)

| Spec | Range |
|---|---|
| Fabric weight | 50–536 GSM |
| Light blocking | 5%–100% |

These vary by collection; do not average or apply the range to a specific fabric without checking that fabric's own spec.

## Header / Hanging Styles

Grommet Top, Back Tab, Pinch Pleat (Double or Triple), Rod Pocket, Flat Panel, Ripple Fold, Euro Pleat (Double or Triple), Tab Top, Pencil Pleat (offered on most but not all collections), and Other/custom. Full specs, hardware compatibility, and stack-back by style: see [Header Style](../guides/header-style.md) and [Stack Back](../guides/stack-back.md).

## Lining Options

Unlined, White Lining, Cotton Lining, Blackout Lining, White Blackout Lining, Triple-Weave Lining, Black Lining, and (on select flame-retardant collections) FR White Lining. Full specs (GSM, color, light-blocking ratio, care): see [Lining Type](../guides/lining-type.md).

## Hardware

Wesley Custom Curtain Tracks span roughly 10"–720" and are rated for heavyweight fabrics — see [Hardware](hardware.md).

## Fullness Guidance

| Look | Recommended Fullness |
|---|---|
| Flat, minimal | 1.5x |
| Standard pleated (most common) | 2.0x |
| Rich pleats / sheers | 2.0x–2.5x |
| Bay windows | 2.0x–2.5x |
