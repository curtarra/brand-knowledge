## Mounting & Lift

Wall or ceiling mount. Lift is cordless or motorized depending on the design.

## Fabric Weight & Light Blocking

| Spec | Range |
|---|---|
| Fabric weight | 160–660 GSM |
| Light blocking | 50%–100% |

## Fire Safety Certification (verified — specific lines only)

Two specific lines are documented as tested to the **NFPA 701** flame-resistance standard:
- **Arthur** — Blackout Flame Resistant Roller Shades
- **Leo** — Custom Weave Solar Shades (also UV-blocking and heat-reducing)

This certification is confirmed for these two named lines only. Do not extend it to other roller shade products without checking that specific product's own documentation.

## Safety Certification

Curtarra states its shades are cordless and meet the ANSI/WCMA A100.1-2018 safety standard.
