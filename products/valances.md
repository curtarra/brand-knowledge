# Valances

## Customizable Range

| Dimension | Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Width | Product pages default to showing 360" as the max — a display convention, not a hard ceiling for solid fabric (splice seam added over 104"; see [Custom Curtains](custom-curtains.md)) | Panels near/above 360" are heavier to handle/hang |
| Length | Up to 36" | Beyond 36" is available but requires a sewn trace/seam and starts to cover more of the window than a valance typically should |

## Fabric Weight & Light Blocking

| Spec | Range |
|---|---|
| Fabric weight | 385–478 GSM |
| Light blocking | 40%–90% |

Valances are decorative toppers, typically layered over a functional curtain or shade rather than used alone for privacy/light control.
