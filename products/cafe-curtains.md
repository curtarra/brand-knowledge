# Café Curtains

## Customizable Range

| Dimension | Range |
|---|---|
| Width | Product pages default to showing 360" as the max — a display convention, not a hard ceiling for solid fabric (splice seam added over 104"). See [Custom Curtains](custom-curtains.md). |
| Length | Up to 104" standard; up to 240" on select fabrics (rotated weave) |

## Fabric Weight & Light Blocking

| Spec | Range |
|---|---|
| Fabric weight | 75–339 GSM |
| Light blocking | 5%–50% |

Café curtains use lighter-weight fabrics than full-length curtains, consistent with their short-drop use on the lower half of a window or glazed door.
