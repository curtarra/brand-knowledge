# Bamboo & Woven Wood Shades

## Materials

Natural-fiber blends including jute, kenaf, mulberry silk, ramie, and bamboo — composition varies by design (e.g. 100% jute, 100% bamboo, 95% kenaf / 5% mulberry silk).

## Customizable Range

| Dimension | Range |
|---|---|
| Width | Up to 95" (some narrower designs cap around 47") |
| Length | Up to 157.5" |

## Mounting & Lift

No-drill mount only across this line. Lift is cordless, or cordless/motorized depending on the design.

## Fabric Weight & Light Blocking

| Spec | Range |
|---|---|
| Fabric weight | 230–1,174 GSM (naturally heavier than fabric shades) |
| Light blocking | 10%–50% (woven construction is light-filtering by nature, not blackout) |
