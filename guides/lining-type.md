# Curtain Lining Type

Lining is an extra layer of fabric sewn to the back of a curtain panel, made-to-order (curtains are unlined by default). Curtarra offers 7 named linings on the public lining guide, plus one specialty fire-resistant option available on select flame-retardant collections.

| Lining | Color | Material | Weight | Light-Blocking (fabric alone) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Unlined | — | — | — | Same as base fabric | Only recommended for sheers or fabrics that are already 100% blackout on their own (e.g. Gary, Todd) |
| White Lining | White | Polyester | 140 GSM | 30% | Seamless back look; adds warmth and sun protection |
| Cotton Lining | White | 100% Cotton (5% shrinkage — dry clean recommended) | 137 GSM | 30% | Same use case as White Lining; more breathable, hypoallergenic |
| Black Lining | Black | Polyester | 140 GSM | 70% | Room-darkening; not recommended for sheer/lightweight light-colored curtains (dark show-through) |
| Triple-Weave Lining | Off-white | Polyester | 220 GSM | 60% | Thermal/insulating + noise-reducing; feels thick |
| Blackout Lining | Grayish white | Polyester, foam-coated | 120 GSM | 100% | Inner side is grayish — can visibly change the look of sheer/light-blocking-under-50% curtains; dry clean only |
| White Blackout Lining | White | Polyester, triple-coated | 240 GSM | 100% | Same 100% blackout as above, but face stays white-on-white; dry clean only |
| FR White Lining (special) | White | — | — | — | Fire-resistant lining variant used only on select flame-retardant collections — not listed on the public lining guide; confirm availability per SKU |

## Combined Light-Blocking Ratio (fabric + lining)

| Lining | Over a Sheer | Over a Drape |
|---|---|---|
| White / Cotton Lining | 30–40% | 40–99% |
| Black Lining | 70–80% | 70–99% |
| Triple-Weave Lining | 60–70% | 60–99% |
| Blackout / White Blackout Lining | 100% | 100% |

Final ratio always depends on the base fabric's own light-blocking property — these are combined effects, not the lining alone.

## Mapping to Shopify's Functional Lining Categories

Product pages also tag curtains with a simpler functional category (`unlined` / `privacy-lined` / `room-darkening-lined` / `blackout-lined` / `thermal-lined`). Approximate mapping:

- White Lining / Cotton Lining → privacy-lined
- Black Lining → room-darkening-lined
- Triple-Weave Lining → thermal-lined
- Blackout Lining / White Blackout Lining → blackout-lined

## Construction Notes

- Lining is sewn on 3 sides (top, left, right) by default; unsewn-bottom is standard so the lining can be trimmed/adjusted. Contact support to specify otherwise.
- Finished lining length runs ~1 1/4" shorter than the finished curtain length (industry standard).
- Any lined curtain is recommended to dry clean; if machine washing, wash gently and one panel at a time.
