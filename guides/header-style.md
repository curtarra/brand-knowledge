# Curtain Header Style

## Grommet
- Look: crisp, contemporary. Even number of grommets for uniform folds.
- Grommet size: I/D 1.6", O/D 2.2". Fits rods under 1.5" diameter. Colors: silver, black, bronze, gold.
- Fold depth 6"; panel projects 3 1/4" from wall; add 1.2" to total length for fabric above the grommets.
- No clips/rings needed. Smallest stack back (~1/8 of panel width). Fullness 1.5–2.5x. Hardware compatibility: see the table below.

## Rod Pocket
- Look: tailored, lined on top.
- Pocket 2.8" H + 1.2" ruffled top = 4" H overall. Fits rods under 1.78" diameter. No-top option available (specify at checkout).
- Bigger pockets and double pocket (for French doors) available on request.
- Rods only, not tracks. Flat (1x) or pleated (1.5–2.5x). Not recommended for heavy/long curtains opened frequently.

## Pinch Pleat (Double / Triple)
- Look: formal, elegant.
- Double: 2 folds, 2x inbuilt fullness. Triple: 3 folds, 2.5x inbuilt fullness.
- Pleat spacing 5"; return/overlap 2.5"; pleats gathered 4" below top.
- Pleat count = panel width ÷ 5" spacing, + 1. Measure panel length from ~2" below rod top (ring diameter).
- Free white adjustable hooks included. Works with rods or tracks + rings/clips (ordered separately). Stack back ≈ 1/5 of panel width.

## Euro Pleat (Double / Triple)
- Look: vintage-meets-contemporary.
- Double: 2 folds, 2x fullness. Triple: 3 folds, 2.5x fullness. Pleat spacing 5"; gathered 1/4" below top.
- Pleat count = panel width ÷ 5" spacing, + 1.
- Works with rods or tracks + rings/clips (ordered separately). Comes with adjustable hooks pre-wrapped. Stack back ≈ 1/5 of panel width.

## Pencil Pleat
- Look: traditional, tightly gathered, semi-formal.
- 2x inbuilt fullness. Pleat height 3 1/2".
- Hook count = panel width ÷ 3" spacing, + 1. Measure from ring hole (~2" from rod top) to floor.
- Works with rods or tracks + rings/runners (ordered separately). Comes with hooks.

## Back Tab
- Look: hidden tabs, soft/casual.
- Tab size 4"H × 1.5"W, one tab per 6 1/2" of width. Fits rods under 2" diameter (rods over 2.5" may not slide smoothly).
- Panel length measured rod-top to floor. Not easy to open/close often — best for lightweight, mostly-stationary curtains. Fullness 1–2x.

## Flat Panel
- Look: functional, modern — 4-in-1 tape gives 4 hanging options: Flat Hooks, Flat Panel (clips), Back Tab, or Pencil Pleat, all on the same panel.
- Free metal hooks included; rings/clips not included, ordered separately.
- Works with rods alone or rods + clip rings/rings/hooks; tracks with carriers also fit. Fullness 1.5–2.5x.

## Tab Top
- Look: casual, rustic/transitional.
- Tab size 4"H × 1.57"W, one tab per 6 1/2" of width. Fits rods under 2" diameter.
- The 4" tab height is included in panel length. Best for sheers and curtains that don't open/close often. Fullness 1.5–2x.

## Ripple Fold
- Look: continuous S-curve wave.
- 3 snap-space options: 4 1/4" (1.8x fullness, ~80%), 5" (2.1x fullness, ~110% — most common), 5 7/10" (2.4x fullness, ~140%). Runner spacing 2.36".
- Default setup (5" tape + 2.36" runner) gives 2.1x fullness and fits ~80% of 3/4"-diameter tracks.
- Panel width = track width ÷ panel quantity (flat panel width ≈ 2.1× that). Panel length measured track-bottom to floor.
- Buckle tape must match buckle runner/carrier; button tape must match button runner/carrier — confirm compatibility if the customer already owns a track/runner system.

## Special / Custom Header Requests
Goblet Pleat, Inverted Box Pleat, Single Pleat, bed canopy, and valance-specific headers are available on request via support@curtarra.com — not standard checkout options.
