# Curtarra

Curtarra is a direct-to-consumer brand selling made-to-measure curtains, shades, and window-treatment hardware, positioned around the idea of "Custom Made Easy."

## Mission

Curtarra's stated mission is to make custom window treatments accessible: offering a level of craftsmanship comparable to a high-end design house, at a more transparent and accessible price than traditional made-to-measure providers.

## Product Philosophy

- Made-to-measure, not off-the-shelf: every panel is cut to the customer's own dimensions rather than sold in fixed standard sizes.
- Free fabric swatches (up to 10 per order) let customers verify color and texture before ordering.
- A free online Measurement Tool and step-by-step guides support customers through sizing without requiring professional installation help.

## History

Curtarra was founded in 2018.
