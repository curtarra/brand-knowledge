# Curtain Hardware FAQ

**Do curtains come with rings, clips, or hooks?**
Rings and clips are always sold separately. Pinch Pleat, Euro Pleat, and Flat Panel headers ship with free white adjustable hooks included; metal pins are available on request at checkout instead.

**How many rings do I need for a pinch/euro pleat curtain?**
Panel width ÷ 5" pleat spacing, + 1. Example: a 25"-wide panel needs 6 rings (25/5 + 1 = 6).

**What rod diameter fits the grommet headers?**
Grommets are 1.6" inner diameter and fit rods up to 1.5" in diameter. Available in silver, black, bronze, gold.

**Do curtain rods come with mounting brackets?**
Yes — 2 brackets for shorter rods, 3 for longer rods.

**Does a curtain wand come with the curtains?**
No, sold separately. To use: hook the wand's metal end onto a ring near the panel edge, then slide it along the rod to open/close.

**Do curtains come with a tieback?**
Yes, free, for most standard curtains at least 24"W × 60"L. Sheers and valances do not include a tieback by default (available on request). To use: wrap the tieback around the panel and connect the loop ends to the metal hooks.

**What are the buttons on the side hem for?**
They anchor the tieback and are removable — squeeze the middle and unplug from the back.

**Can curtains be hung on a track system?**
Yes — Pinch Pleat is the best-suited header for tracks, and includes free hooks/pins.
