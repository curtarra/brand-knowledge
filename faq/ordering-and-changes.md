# Ordering & Order Changes FAQ

**How do I place an order?**
Choose a fabric on curtarra.com, then follow the on-page customization steps (size, header style, lining, quantity) through checkout. The How To Customize guide on curtarra.com walks through the process step by step.

**Can I modify my order (size, lining, etc.) after placing it?**
Yes, if it hasn't entered shipping. Contact support@curtarra.com or LiveChat. Price differences are refunded or invoiced. Once shipped, nothing can be modified.

**What if I ordered the wrong size or color?**
Contact support before the order is made or shipped for a fix. Once finished/shipped, custom goods can't be cancelled, returned, or reused for another customer — but a 50% discount is offered on a corrected second order if placed within 30 calendar days of receiving the first.

**I applied the wrong coupon code — can it be fixed?**
Email service@curtarra.com or use LiveChat with the order number, the wrong code used, and the correct code. The price difference is refunded once confirmed valid.

**Checkout or payment won't go through — what now?**
Send the order details (product, color, header style, size, lining, design, quantity) or a screenshot to support; the team can issue a PayPal invoice as a workaround. Otherwise try a different card or payment method.

**Can I send my own fabric to be made into curtains?**
Not recommended — production happens at the factory in China, so the customer would pay to ship fabric there and face a longer turnaround.

**Can I order fabric only (no finished curtain)?**
Yes, but it's handled directly by support@curtarra.com rather than standard checkout.
