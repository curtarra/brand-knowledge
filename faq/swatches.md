# Free Swatches FAQ

**Are swatches really free?**
The swatches themselves are free; a $3.99 shipping fee applies for up to 10 swatches per order. Free shipping applies to custom-made curtain/shade/hardware orders; swatch/sample orders are a different product category and carry this small fee. See [Shipping Policy](../policies/shipping.md).

**How long do swatches take to arrive?**
7–10 business days in the US.

**Can I order more than 10?**
Not in one order — place a separate order for additional swatches.

**A swatch won't add to my cart / shows out of stock — what do I do?**
It's temporarily unavailable. Order the other swatches you want first, then email support the one that's unavailable — it will ship together from the factory, delayed by about 7 business days.

**Why might the swatch color not exactly match the product photo?**
Printing batch differences and screen brightness/resolution vary. Order a swatch first if color accuracy matters for the final decision.
