# Return & Refund Policy

## Order Modifications

Customization parameters (size, color, header, lining, etc.) can be modified any time **before the order ships**. Any price difference is refunded or invoiced accordingly. Once shipped, no modifications are possible. See [Ordering & Order Changes FAQ](../faq/ordering-and-changes.md).

## Order Cancellations

- Full-refund cancellation: available within 48 hours of ordering, if unshipped.
- Cancellation after 48 hours (still unshipped): 70% refund.
- Once an order ships, it can't be cancelled, returned, or refunded for a change of mind.

## Customer Measurement Errors

If the customer's own measurements were inaccurate, Curtarra can adjust the width for a fee, or offer a 50% discount on a replacement order placed within 30 calendar days of receiving the original. Return shipping in this case is paid by the customer.

## Not Satisfied (Correctly Made, No Defect)

Within 30 days of receipt, customers unhappy with a correctly made custom order can go through Curtarra's donation process (donate to Goodwill, provide a receipt) to become eligible for a partial refund. Customers should contact Curtarra before donating.

## Manufacturing or Material Defects

Covered under Curtarra's Lifetime Limited Warranty: confirmed defects are repaired or replaced free of charge (closest available alternative if the original fabric is discontinued). Return shipping for confirmed quality issues is paid by Curtarra.

## Non-Custom Items

Select adjustable hardware (rods, wands) and accessories (rings, clips) in original packaging may be returned; customer pays return shipping. No restocking fee.

## Not Eligible for Return

- Swatch / sample booklets
- Normal wear and tear

## Refund Timing

Once a return is approved, the refund is submitted internally within 24 hours; funds typically post to the original payment method within 1–15 business days, depending on the issuing bank.
