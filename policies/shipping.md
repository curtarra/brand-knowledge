# Shipping Policy

## Delivery Time

Custom items (curtains, shades, rods, tracks, etc.) are made to order, so delivery has two parts: processing (production) + shipping (transit).

| | Ordinary Day | Holiday Season |
|---|---|---|
| Processing Time | 7–10 business days | 13–35 business days |
| Shipping Time | 7–10 business days | 7–10 business days |
| **Total Time** | **14–20 business days** | **20–45 business days** |

- Free swatches arrive in 7–10 business days in the US.
- Delivery to Canada takes 10–15 business days.
- This policy covers deliveries to the US and Canada. Shipping to other destinations is not documented here.
- During the Chinese New Year period (January–February), delivery time may be extended by up to 45 business days.
- Watch for the shipping notification email for order-specific timing.

## Shipping Fee

- **FREE shipping** on custom-made products (curtains, shades, hardware) across the US and Canada.
- **Sample/swatch orders**: $3.99 shipping fee for up to 10 swatches per order (see [Free Swatches FAQ](../faq/swatches.md)).

These are two different product categories, not a conflicting claim — custom-made orders ship free; swatch/sample orders carry the $3.99 fee.

## Shipping Carrier

- Custom curtain/shade/hardware orders: shipped by air, mainly via FedEx or DHL.
- Free fabric swatches: mostly shipped via USPS.

## Shipping Address

- Fabric swatches can be delivered to PO boxes.
- Curtain and hardware orders **cannot** be delivered to PO boxes.

## Tracking

A tracking number (carrier + number) is emailed once an order ships. Check status via the Track Order page (curtarra.com/pages/track-order) or the carrier's own site.

## Damage or Lost Package

1. Take a photo of the damage (or describe the loss).
2. Report to the carrier:
   - FedEx: 1-800-GoFedEx / 1-800-463-3339 (US & Canada)
   - UPS: 1-800-742-5877 (US & Canada)
   - DHL: 1-800-225-5345 (US); 1-855-345-7447 (Canada)
3. Email support@curtarra.com with the problem description and photos.

Curtarra will help locate or remake confirmed lost/damaged items once the carrier confirms the claim.

## Additional Requests

Expediting, address changes, or other shipping issues: contact support@curtarra.com.
